    name, name

    they call my name

    but, nonetheless

    their attempts are in vain

    the noise in my mind

    does not gain

    i nod at the order

    i bow at the command

    but to a name

    i mustn't nod

    nor bow

    is it x?

    is it y?

    they ask,

    but i dont reply

    for i'd lie, if i were to comply

    to say that their words did not pass me by

    who am i?
